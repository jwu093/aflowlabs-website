Privacy Statement

DwellCheck processes property documents that may contain sensitive personal, financial and property information. This statement explains what we process, why we process it and the choices available to you.

Effective 24 July 2026 · Version 2026-07-24

The short version

  • We use your documents to create the report you request.
  • DwellCheck is not a document vault. We do not intentionally retain original uploaded files in our own long-term document storage after processing by default.
  • Reports stay linked to your account until you delete them or delete your account, subject to limited legal obligations.
  • We may retain de-identified learning signals for a limited time to improve accuracy and safety.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

Information we process

Depending on how you use DwellCheck, we may process:

  • your account name, email address, account identifier and sign-in provider;
  • property documents you choose to upload;
  • property addresses, document facts, findings, scores and generated reports;
  • purchase, entitlement and free-preview records;
  • support requests and messages you send us; and
  • limited device, security, reliability and product-usage information needed to operate and protect the service.

Property documents can contain information about other people, including owners, vendors, purchasers and advisers. Only upload documents you are authorised to use. We do not use the information in those documents to contact those people.

How we use information

We process information to:

  • authenticate and secure your account;
  • analyse uploaded documents and create structured findings, comparisons, scores and reports;
  • restore reports, purchases and access entitlements;
  • operate free-preview limits and prevent misuse;
  • provide support and investigate reliability issues;
  • protect users, DwellCheck and our systems; and
  • improve rule matching, risk calibration, wording and product safety using the limited records described below.

We do not sell personal information or use uploaded property documents for third-party advertising.

Document and AI processing

Documents selected in the app are sent securely to the DwellCheck backend and to our configured AI service provider to produce structured findings. DwellCheck then applies its own knowledge, validation and scoring rules to those findings.

DwellCheck does not intentionally retain original uploaded files in its own long-term document storage after processing by default. Temporary processing copies may exist while a review is being created or handled by our service providers under their applicable service terms and safeguards. Keep your original files because DwellCheck is not a document vault.

Avoid sending documents by email. If you need support, send the report ID and a description of the issue. Do not send a full property document unless we specifically ask for a safe diagnostic extract.

Service providers and overseas processing

We use providers for cloud hosting, databases, authentication, AI processing and payments. These may include AWS, Supabase, OpenAI, Apple and Google where relevant.

Those providers may process information outside New Zealand. We use providers for the services they supply to DwellCheck and require appropriate privacy and security safeguards for personal information they handle. We remain responsible for describing how DwellCheck uses and shares your information.

Reports, learning signals and retention

Generated reports and purchase entitlements remain associated with your account until you delete the report or account, unless we need to retain a record to meet a legal obligation, resolve a dispute or protect the service.

To improve DwellCheck’s accuracy and safety, we may retain de-identified finding signals, short redacted evidence snippets and coarse statistics—such as city, document mix and score band—for up to 24 months. These improvement records are designed not to contain full documents, exact property addresses, account IDs or contact details.

Free-preview misuse prevention

To prevent repeated free previews being obtained by deleting and recreating an account, DwellCheck keeps a short-lived keyed fingerprint derived from the account email and the date the preview was used. The fingerprint does not contain the email, is not linked to documents or reports, is not used for marketing and expires when the free-preview cooldown ends.

Deleting reports or your account

Deleting a report removes the report and its associated access from your account. De-identified improvement records may remain for the limited period described above.

Deleting your account removes the account and account-linked reports, entitlements and profile data, except information we must retain by law, information already de-identified and the short-lived free-preview fingerprint described above.

Security

We use access controls, encrypted network connections, restricted administrative access, private backend credentials and data-minimisation controls designed to protect personal information. No online service can guarantee absolute security.

If we become aware of a privacy breach that has caused or is likely to cause serious harm, we will follow applicable New Zealand notification requirements.

Your privacy rights

You may ask whether we hold personal information about you, request access to it, ask us to correct it, or request deletion where applicable. You may also raise a privacy concern with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner in New Zealand.

DwellCheck is intended for people aged 18 or older and is not designed for children.

Contact us

Aflow Labs Limited is responsible for DwellCheck. For privacy questions or requests, email help@aflowlabs.com. Please do not include full property documents in your first message.

We may update this statement when our service or legal obligations change. Material changes will be identified by a new effective date.
